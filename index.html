About Me

Computational Chemist & Developer

I'm a BS-MS student at IISER Kolkata, specializing in computational chemistry with a minor in computer science. My research interests lie at the intersection of quantum chemistry, machine learning, and information retrieval.

With a strong background in both theoretical chemistry and programming, I develop computational tools and algorithms to solve complex chemical problems, from molecular modeling to electronic structure calculations.

I'm passionate about using computational approaches to understand chemical phenomena at the molecular level and developing novel methodologies for predicting molecular properties and behaviors.

As an educator and mentor, I enjoy sharing knowledge and helping others understand complex concepts in computational chemistry and scientific programming.
